Danny's

Danny’s, an app meticulously crafted to enhance the food truck ordering experience, addresses common challenges users face in pre-ordering from food trucks.


Many struggle with ordering in advance, with this option often unavailable. Additionally, menus may only be accessible in person. Danny’s addresses these issues, offering a solution for users to conveniently pre-order, view menus in advance, and crucially, customize orders based on ingredient preferences or allergies.


Throughout the condensed 3-week timeline, I spearheaded the end-to-end UX of this project, ensuring a comprehensive and user-centric approach for the application.
